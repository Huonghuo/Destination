Ha Long Bay

A UNESCO World Heritage Site. Halong Bay in Vietnam is a renowned natural marvel, featuring thousands of limestone islands and islets emerging from emerald waters. This UNESCO World Heritage Site is a picturesque destination known for its captivating beauty and unique karst landscape. Visitors can explore the bay through boat cruises, kayaking, and cave exploration, immersing themselves in the stunning scenery and rich biodiversity of the area.


Sunworld Ha Long Park

A thrilling amusement park. Sun World Halong Park is a popular amusement park located in Halong Bay, Vietnam. It offers a range of thrilling rides and attractions, making it a perfect destination for families and thrill-seekers alike. From roller coasters to water slides and cultural performances, Sun World Halong Park provides a fun-filled experience that complements the natural beauty of Halong Bay.


Tuan Chau Island

A tropical paradise.Tuan Chau Island is a scenic island located in Halong Bay, Vietnam. It is a popular tourist destination known for its pristine beaches, luxury resorts, and various entertainment activities. Visitors can relax on the sandy shores, enjoy water sports, indulge in delicious seafood, and witness mesmerizing cultural performances, making Tuan Chau Island a perfect getaway within the stunning Halong Bay region
